How It Works:

For Customers:

  • Step 1: Enter your meter number and select your meter type.
  • Step 2: Confirm your details and enter the recharge amount.
  • Step 3: Complete your payment via Paystack.
  • Step 4: Receive your power token instantly.

For Merchants:

  • Step 1: Create a merchant account.
  • Step 2: Log in to your merchant dashboard.
  • Step 3: Enter client meter details and purchase tokens on their behalf.
  • Step 4: Earn commissions on every transaction.
